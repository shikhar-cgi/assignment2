Director Message

Welcome you all to IIT BHU.You are entering at a landmark time point in the history of this institute. IIT (BHU) is celebrating its centenary year in 2019. It’s a matter of pride for us to welcome you all as a “Centenary Batch”.

At IIT(BHU) Varanasi, we are committed to your holistic growth through our evolutionary curriculum. The revised course structure which will be implemented from the upcoming academic session has been devised to help you garner sagacity and acuity through your dispassionate engagement with the core disciplines on one hand, and to pursue subtlety and sensibility through your fervent tryst with the humanities on the other.

Simultaneously, you will be introduced to the vibrant co-curricular and extra-curricular life offering a range of new experiences at IIT(BHU) Varanasi. The Institute has a variety of sports, cultural, social, science and technology, and media platforms to enable you to be yourself while developing a greater understanding of the others around you. At IIT(BHU) Varanasi your classroom experience is intertwined with the demands of the workplace. The Training and Placement Cell of the Institute helps you hone your skills and smoothly transition into the world of work with a deeper understanding of the nuances of life at work.

You are at the verge of walking into the annals of history as students of this great institute. I am sure that you while basking in the glory of being an integral part of this Institute, will take it to new heights making the future generations look up to it with a sense of unassumed admiration.

Director, IIT(BHU) Varanasi.
